Overworld

Privacy Policy

Last updated July 14, 2026

Overworld is designed to keep your exploration data on your device. It has no account system, advertising, analytics, tracking SDK, developer-operated server, or built-in cloud synchronization.

Information Overworld stores

Overworld stores revealed map cells as integer grid coordinates. It does not store raw GPS history, routes, timestamps, speed, contacts, photos, messages, or identifiers used to track you across apps or websites.

Location

Overworld requests Always location access so it can reveal nearby map cells while the app is open, in the background, or while the screen is off. Location fixes are processed on your device and are not transmitted to the developer. You can pause tracking in Overworld or change location access in iOS Settings at any time.

Map data

The app displays Apple Maps through Apple's native MapKit framework. Apple's handling of requests made by its operating-system services is governed by Apple's policies. Overworld does not operate a map server.

Backups and exports

You may export a standard or password-protected .overworld backup. Exports contain revealed grid cells and format metadata, not raw location history. You choose where to save or share exported files. Password-protected exports use authenticated encryption, and Overworld cannot recover forgotten passwords.

The live app database may be included in device backups according to your iOS and iCloud settings. Those backups are managed by Apple, not by Overworld.

Purchases

Overworld Pro purchases are handled by Apple through StoreKit. The developer does not receive your payment-card details. Apple provides the app with entitlement information needed to unlock purchased features.

Data sharing and retention

Because Overworld does not collect your data on a developer-operated server, the developer has no personal map data to sell, share, retain, or delete. You can erase explored areas within the app or delete the app and its local data through iOS.

Children

Overworld does not knowingly collect personal information from children or anyone else.

Changes

If Overworld's data practices change, this policy and the App Store privacy disclosure will be updated before the change is released.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to overworldsupport@icloud.com.
