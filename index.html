Reporting a problem

Please include your iPhone model, iOS version, what you expected, what happened, and any steps that reproduce the problem. Do not send an exported map unless requested.

Overworld Pro

Overworld Pro is a one-time, non-consumable purchase. If a previous purchase is not recognized, open Overworld, go to Settings, select Overworld Pro, and tap Restore Purchases.

Password-protected backups

Overworld cannot recover a forgotten export password. A protected backup cannot be opened without the password used to create it.

Privacy

Overworld has no account, analytics, advertising, or developer-operated server. Read the Overworld Privacy Policy.
